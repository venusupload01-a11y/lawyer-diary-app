Record Pull Setup (Both Cases)
1) Open sync\pull-config.json
2) Paste eCourts case-details URL for:
   - case-main (PWDVA JMFC Court)
   - case-sessions (Session Court - Appeal)
3) Run: Pull Case Records Now.bat
4) Keep app open and click "Sync Records" (or wait auto-sync every 2 minutes)
5) For continuous updates, run: Start Auto Pull Records.bat

Notes:
- Pulled JSON is saved at sync\latest-case-data.json
- Raw fetched HTML snapshots are saved at sync\raw\
